About the Program

Why VC works best in Silicon Valley?
Is targeted investing the best strategy to build big wins?
What parts of the venture capital business are competitive and when is it collaborative?

Blackbox Investor Retreat is an initiative to explore how early stage venture investors from all around the globe might collaborate and share best practices to support ambitious entrepreneurs build great companies and increase the success rate and return size of their investments. While in Silicon Valley, a small group of participants will have a chance to meet with successful veteran Venture Capitalists, Angels Investors, Legal Experts and Thought Leaders.

Who should attend?

  • Fund General & Limited Partners
  • Family Office Funds
  • Angel investors
  • High net-worth individuals with interest in investing with high performing Silicon Valley venture capital funds
  • Public Funds Professionals who are interested in promoting entrepreneurship in their communities
