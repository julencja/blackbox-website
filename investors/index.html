Blackbox Investor Retreat is an exclusive and selctive gathering of global early stage investors in Atherton, California. For 5 days a small group of qualified international investors, limited to maximum 2 participants from each country, will get a unique opportunity to learn:

  1. how to select high quality startups to invest in
  2. how to structure their collaboration with a startup to maximize the ROI
  3. how to add value to a portfolio startup team at the post-investment stage
  4. how to exit from early stage investments
  5. how to access deal flow and co-invest with SIlicon Valley venture capital community

You will have a unique chance to learn from successful Silicon Valley veterans: Venture Capitalists, Angels Investors, Serial Entrepreneurs and Legal Experts and other insiders who make up the ecosystem.

What should you expect?

  • No suits and ties allowed: visit top Silicon Valley VC firms and meet with successful experienced investment partners.
  • Keep it exclusive: expert presentations on global investment and tax laws in a private backyard of the Blackbox Mansion.
  • Share your experience: straight talks with other international investors from around the globe about various opportunities in different hubs and regions.
  • Build relationships: socialize and break bread with selected network of Silicon Valley insiders
  • Keep it real: get a full taste of the Blackbox Mansion experience as real successful entrepreneurs do.

The goal of the program is to improve your ROI by adopting some of Silicon Valley VC best practices and to learn what it takes to help your portfolio companies expand and do business in SV and the US market.

Retreat is limited to no more than two investors from each country (or regions in some cases)
