Blackbox Investor Retreat is a private, exclusive, and invitation-only gathering of global early stage investors in Atherton, California. The purpose is to build and strengthen their relationships with Silicon Valley’s venture capital community.

The participants, limited to maximum 2 from each country, will explore how they might collaborate and share best practices to increase the success rate and return size of their investments while supporting ambitious entrepreneurs in building great companies.

They will meet with successful veteran Venture Capitalists, Angels Investors, Legal Experts and Opinion Makers, and have a unique opportunity to build significant relationships with the top Silicon Valley Business, Technology, and Thought Leaders.

Why VC works best in Silicon Valley?
Is targeted investing the best strategy to build big wins?
What parts of the venture capital business are competitive and when is it collaborative?

Who should attend?

  • Fund General & Limited Partners
  • Family Office Funds
  • Angel investors
  • High net-worth individuals with interest in investing with high performing Silicon Valley venture capital funds
  • Public Funds Professionals who are interested in promoting entrepreneurship in their communities

Why you don't want to miss it?

The goal of this event is to improve ROI by adopting some of Silicon Valley VC best practices in a unique environment, where sharing experience, challenging one’s own knowledge, and building relationships with like minded investors benefits everyone.

  • Get insights on how top performing VCs operate and interface with their portfolio companies as they grow them to $B exits.
  • Build relationships with local and global VCs to syndicate capital, and increase deal flow quality and quantity.
  • Learn what it takes to help your portfolio companies expand and do business in SV and the US market.

What should you expect?

  • Visits to Silicon Valley top VC firms and meet with successful experienced investment partners. No suits and ties allowed.
  • Round Table discussions – bean bags and couches – with other international investors from around the globe about various opportunities in different hubs and regions.
  • Expert presentations on global investment and tax laws in a private backyard of the Blackbox Mansion.
  • Socialize and break bread with select network of Silicon Valley insiders – beer and bbq.
  • Get a full taste of the Blackbox Mansion experience (option for sleeping in shared rooms on bunk beds as our star entrepreneurs do) and keep it real.
