The Story

Wappwolf

The Product

Wappwolf is building the next virtual filesystem on top of cloud services and cloud storages. The first product with 85,000 users was the Wappwolf Automator for Facebook, Dropbox, Google Drive and box to keep your services in sync and automate repetitive tasks like converting filetypes or uploading to other services.

Now the company is working on the next step following the vision – iBeam.it – seamless sharing with a publish/subscribe concept, a new way to publish your content and files to your friends using their favorite choice of service or storage to subscribe.

mykoob.com

The Story

Girts and Martins founded MyKoob in Latvia with the goal of improving the level of parents involvements in their children's education. They have won an award at one of the Pioneers Festival in 2011 and came to explore what SV might have for them. They joined Blackbox Connect along with 9 other international startups in May of 2012. They've since set up HQ in US, signed up a network of 900 private schools in the US and raised a significant round of capital from Nestadio Capital in France and Baltic VC and added two new team members.

The Product

With MyKoob, Schools (K-12) get easy to use tools to optimize and manage their operations as well as automating the communication regarding the daily school work between students, parents and teachers. With a simple business model, MyKoob quickly gained more than 60% of the Latvian market.

The Story

fidointelligence.com

The Story

Michal and Tom participated in the first Blackbox Connect in November 2011, and were quickly connected with top advisors and domain experts who keep helping them with the company's mission to teach computers grammar so they can understand humans.

Fido has taken the "path less traveled" as a deep IP technology company with a goal to disrupt the Language Understanding space. The CEO – a doctor of psychiatry – has built a team of mathematicians, quantum physicists, linguists, computer scientists, philosophers and business operation fanatics. Well known in Europe for creating first of its kind innovation in Natural Language Processing for business, (SIRI-like applications, Email Processors) and society (e-learning with language understanding and child safety monitoring systems).

Technology

Language Decoder is a technology that converts any text into a logical structure a sequence of verb-related functions with parameters containing syntactic and semantic information. The company is currently in stealth mode.
