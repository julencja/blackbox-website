Transport


Taxis

There are taxi cards on the cork-board at the front door. One good choice for the Atherton area is: Edgar Taxi (650)669-2314. They are based very close by in Atherton/Menlo Park. They are 24-hour and know where we are. (Tell them Blackbox on Selby in Atherton). They can get also you from the airport if your destination is Blackbox. Please keep in mind that they prefer payment in cash and some foreign credit cards may not work. For other companies, see the list at the front door.

Rail

Nearby is the Caltrain: http://www.caltrain.com. Nearest stations are Atherton (20 minutes walk, but does NOT operate on weekdays), Redwood City and Menlo Park. Caltrain connects to the BART rail system that connects San Francisco with San Bruno, Millbrae, Oakland and Berkeley.

Rental cars

When looking at the "whole picture", many visitors often find renting a car to be a requirement but it's not useful for the participants of the Connect program. Usually you should pay around $30/day to rent a car. It's good and cheaper to rent a car straight at the airport. Better book your in advance, using eg. Hotwire, Kayak, or Expedia.

Traditional car-rental options close to Blackbox:
  • Enterprise (very close to Blackbox, on El Camino Real)
  • U-Haul (Redwood City)
  • Hertz (Redwood City)

Non-traditional car rentals, car sharing and pay-per-hour car rentals:
  • Zipcar
  • City Car Share
  • Uber

Bicycles
