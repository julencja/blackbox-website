How do we work with you?

Blackbox Residence »

When we don't have a program running, you may come here and stay with us at Blackbox for up to 4 weeks.

Treat this as an opportunity to make your very first Silicon Valley connections, and live and work among the people similar to you.

» How to stay here?

Blackbox Connect »

Two to three times a year we run a structured 2-week residential program for just 12 outstanding international founders.

You get through an application process, pay a program fee and stay at the Blackbox Mansion, where for 2 weeks you take part in an intensive Silicon Valley brainwash full of workshops, lectures, networking events, and meetings with investors and serial entrepreneurs.

You'll dive into Silicon Valley and get recognition in its ecosystem.

» How to apply?

Blackbox Accelerate »

If we both believe we can help you, we commit to accelerate your growth for no less than 1 year.

This means that you join the global Blackbox community, stay at the Blackbox Mansion, get legal & administrative help to set up a US presence of your company, recruit and build global core startup team, and raise investment capital.

With the value that we provide we earn small equity in your company and align our interests long term.

» How to get in?

Why are we doing this?

Entrepreneurship is all about self-leadership.

Success of a founder is not a matter of funding, or skills, or diplomas — it's all about how well he knows himself and expresses himself to the world in a way that creates value.

The ones who succeed are the ones who are clear about who they are and what drives them. The ones who cure their own pains with extremely strong motivation.

If you're a purposeful driven individual who wants to use or invest in technology to improve lives, I want to hear from you.

Fadi Bishara Blackbox Founder

Fadi has been working with technology entrepreneurs in Silicon Valley since 1996, recruiting and building core teams for over 100 early stage startups, such us Lala (Apple), Tapulous (Disney), Zynga (IPO), Splunk (IPO), nVidia (NVDA), Four11 (Yahoo!), X10SW (Oracle), VMWare (IPO), Bebo (AOL), PeakStream (Google), SixApart, VideoEgg, Splunk, Blurb, MerchantCircle, and many others.

With over 20 years of experience as a the CEO of TechVenture, Inc., Fadi has a deep understanding of real daily problems of an early stage startup.

He's the author of the Ergonomic Hiring method and a sought after speaker, appearing at conferences and universities such as Stanford, UCLA, Moscow Institute of Physics and Technology, Open University Skolkovo Foundation, St. Petersburg National Research University, Singularity University and the Presidio Graduate School of Management. He served two years as an Executive Committee Co-Chair of the MIT/Stanford Venture Lab and is currently an Advisory Board Member there.

Fadi started Blackbox in 2010, with a vision to build a global ecosystem to nurture ambitious change makers and innovators no matter where they live or work.
