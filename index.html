How do we work with you?

Blackbox Residence

» How to stay here?

When we don't have a program running, you may come here and stay with us at Blackbox for up to 4 weeks.

Treat this as an opportunity to make your very first Silicon Valley connections, and live and work among the people similar to you.

or

Blackbox Connect

» How to apply?

Two to three times a year we run a structured 2-week residential program for just 12 outstanding international founders.

You get through an application process, pay a program fee and stay in the Blackbox Mansion, where for 2 weeks you take part an in intensive Silicon Valley brainwash full of workshops, lectures, networking events, and meetings with investors and serial entrepreneurs.

You'll dive into Silicon Valley and get recognition in its ecosystem.

and

Blackbox Accelerate

» How to get in?

Next, if we believe we can help you grow, we offer to accelerate you for a period of up to 2 years.

This means that we will let you live and work at Blackbox, and help you set up business in the US, raise funding, take care of your legal and admin stuff, and get you growing here.

In exchange for custom-tailored acceleration, we will ask for 2-5% of your company.

We will plug you into our network of mentors and advisors who will help you get from the idea to a growing business.

Why are we doing this?

Because we believe that raising successful startups is not about just investing in batches of entrepreneurs. Nor we think that “high-growth potential” stands for fancy product ideas and sophisticated business plans.

We have learned that the ones who succeed are the ones who are clear about who they are and what drives them. The ones who cure their own pains with extremely strong motivation.

Our role is to provide you with couching, support, and the best possible resources you'll need in order to succeed.
