How do we work with you?

Blackbox
Residence

If you are an entrepreneur visiting Silicon Valley, Blackbox Mansion is the best place for you to stay and make your first connections.

Residence is exclusive to entrepreneurs. Priority is given to people referred by our Partners.

Learn more »

Blackbox
Connect

For 2 weeks 12 international founders who want to dive into Silicon Valley's ecosystem stay together in the Blackbox Mansion where they learn from, and connect with, a curated network of thought leaders, investors and successful entrepreneurs.

Learn more »

Blackbox
Accelerate

If we both believe we can help you, we commit to accelerate your growth for no less than 1 year.

With the value that we provide we earn small equity in your company and align our interests long term.

Who do we work with?

We focus on people, first.

We work only with people who care about the problems they solve. We don't get too attached to your product idea, nor we expect you to do so.

Instead, we want you to show us what it is you want to cure that pains you, and why you are the best person to implement the cure.

Why are we doing this?

Fadi Bishara Blackbox Founder

Fadi has been working with technology entrepreneurs in Silicon Valley since 1996, recruiting and building core teams for over 100 early stage startups, such us Lala (Apple), Tapulous (Disney), Zynga (IPO), Splunk (IPO), Inktomi (Yahoo!), Bebo (AOL), Apture (Google), PeakStream (Google), LiveJournal, VideoEgg, Blurb, Change.org, and many others.

With over 20 years of experience as a the CEO of TechVenture, Inc., Fadi has a deep understanding of real daily problems of an early stage startup.

Fadi started Blackbox in 2010, with a vision to build a global ecosystem to nurture ambitious change makers and innovators no matter where they live or work.
