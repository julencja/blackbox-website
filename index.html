How do we work with you?

Blackbox
Residence

Live and work among the community of like-minded founders, get feedback about your product or vision, meet and talk to Silicon Valley industry experts.

Who is it for?

International entrepreneurs visiting Silicon Valley who want to make their very first connections here.

Learn more »

Blackbox
Connect

Half-a-year Silicon Valley experience condensed in a two‐week immersion program. Learn the DNA of Silicon Valley and prepare to bring your company to the next, global level.

Who is it for?

Non-US startups that want to scale globally, establish US-based business operations, and seek to raise funding in Silicon Valley.

Learn more »

Blackbox
Accelerate

Custom acceleration for no less than a year, with the objective to get your company funded and establish your business operations in the US.

Who is it for?

Blackbox Connect Alumni, former Blackbox Residents, or companies referred by the Referring Partners.

Learn more »

Who do we work with?

We focus on people, first.

We work only with people who care about the problems they solve. We don't get too attached to your product idea, nor we expect you to do so. Instead, we want you to show us what it is you want to cure that pains you, and why you are the best person to implement the cure.

Why are we doing this?

Fadi Bishara Blackbox Founder

Fadi has been working with technology entrepreneurs in Silicon Valley since 1996, recruiting and building core teams for over 100 early stage startups, such us Lala (Apple), Tapulous (Disney), Zynga (IPO), Splunk (IPO), Inktomi (Yahoo!), Bebo (AOL), Apture (Google), PeakStream (Google), LiveJournal, VideoEgg, Blurb, Change.org, and many others.

With over 20 years of experience as a the CEO of TechVenture, Inc., Fadi has a deep understanding of real daily problems of an early stage startup.

Fadi started Blackbox in 2010, with a vision to build a global ecosystem to nurture ambitious change makers and innovators no matter where they live or work.
