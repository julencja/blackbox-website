Blackbox Mansion

Unique Community and Live+Work Space for Entrepreneurs in the Heart of Silicon Valley

Why stay here?

If you are an entrepreneur visiting Silicon Valley, Blackbox Mansion is the best place for you to stay and make your very first connections here.

We host regular events and workshops with serial entrepreneurs, experts and investors. Living in Blackbox during your stay in Silicon Valley gives you a unique opportunity to build relationships and share your vision with people similar to you.

Blackbox Mansion is available exclusively for entrepreneurs. Unless you are a company we accelerate, you can stay here for up to 3 weeks.

In order to stay in Blackbox Mansion, you need to book it through AirBnB. Booking priority is given to people referred by our Referral Partners or Blackbox Ambassadors.

What's it like?

  • Stay in the community of people similar to you
  • Have a constant opportunity to discuss your challenges with other entrepreneurs
  • Meet industry experts attending frequent Blackbox events
  • Get feedback about your project from like-minded founders
  • Contribute to the global community of entrepreneurs by sharing your know-how and experience
