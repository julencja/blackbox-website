mdot.it

The Story

Domink and Pavel are came to Blackbox just to stay here for their YCombinator interviews. Although they got rejected by YC, they decided to stay longer and work with us at Blackbox to help them with their vision. In less than 6 months they refined their product, set up US corporation, raised money from Keith Teare of Archimedes Labs (and Co-Founder of TechCrunch), Mike Maples of Floodgate, Ron Conway's SVAngels, and others. They hired 3 people back in the Czech Republic and just launched their product.

The Product

With m.dot all the business owners can establish, manage, and promote their whole mobile presence using just a smartphone, no coding or design required.

mykoob.com

The Story

Girts and Martins founded MyKoob in Latvia with the goal of improving the level of parents involvements in their children's education. They have won an award at one of the Pioneers Festival in 2011 and came to explore what SV might have for them. They joined Blackbox Connect along with 9 other international startups in May of 2012. They've since set up HQ in US, signed up a network of 900 private schools in the US and raised a significant round of capital from Nestadio Capital in France and Baltic VC and added two new team members.

The Product

With MyKoob, Schools (K-12) get easy to use tools to optimize and manage their operations as well as automating the communication regarding the daily school work between students, parents and teachers. With a simple business model, MyKoob quickly gained more than 60% of the Latvian market.

empreware.com

The Story

Agustin came to SV to participate in Blackbox Connect in April 2012. It's where they fine tuned their product and business strategy with the knowledge they gained from their initial (profitable) Business Planning App with customers in 20 different Spanish speaking countries. With the knowledge they gained, they also connected with Dave McClure of 500 Startups and became an investor in Empreware joining other US and Latin America investors such as Michael De La Maza and Ariel Arieta & Gonzalo Costa of Nxtp.Labs. Since then, they have launched a new product, grew the team and are building ties with great network of US partners and investors laying the grounds for significant series A round to scale and take on the 30 million SMBs in Latin America.

The Product

Empreware helps small businesses to manage their accounts receivable and get paid faster. They started with a Business Planning App that grow fast to help over 180,000 users (12,000 paying customers) in the $3.8B Spanish SMBs market.

fidointelligence.com

The Story

Michal and Tom participated in the first Blackbox Connect in November 2011, and were quickly connected with top advisors and domain experts who keep helping them with the company's mission to teach computers grammar so they can understand humans.

Fido has taken the "path less traveled" as a deep IP technology company with a goal to disrupt the Language Understanding space. The CEO – a doctor of psychiatry – has built a team of mathematicians, quantum physicists, linguists, computer scientists, philosophers and business operation fanatics. Well known in Europe for creating first of its kind innovation in Natural Language Processing for business, (SIRI-like applications, Email Processors) and society (e-learning with language understanding and child safety monitoring systems).

Technology

Language Decoder is a technology that converts any text into a logical structure a sequence of verb-related functions with parameters containing syntactic and semantic information. The company is currently in stealth mode.

solvr.io

The Story

Lars is a software developer, mathematician and entrepreneur. He specializes in Linux kernel, java and C# programmer and has over 15 years of experience. First employee at SoftScan, the security company that exited to Symantec in 2009. He came to Blacbox in June 2012, initially for 2 weeks but eventually stayed for 3 months and took part in Blackbox Connect Program in July.

appstract.me

The Story

Martin and Kim came to Silicon Valley in November 2012 for two weeks to learn more about the way of the Valley after having made a soft beta launch of appstract.me a couple of months earlier. In February 2013 they participated in Blackbox Connect to tune their market approach and got a lot of useful feedback. They are currently working on fine tuning appstract.me for mobile and tablets and are working on setting up a US HQ during the second half of 2013.

The Product

appstract.me is an operating system in the cloud built purely on open technologies. The aim is to free you from the attachment to a physical device, being able to access all your content and applications from any device directly from a browser.
