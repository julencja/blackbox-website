At Blackbox, you'll discover the secret sauce of Silicon Valley: its people. During your two weeks stay at Blackbox Mansion, you'll have the opportunity to live among and collaborate with like-minded entrepreneurs and startups founders from all around the world as well as partake in an intense agenda of meetings and workshops with inspiring serial entrepreneurs, investors, experts and executives.

How to apply?

If you have founded a startup outside of the US and you seek to establish US-based business operations or launch globally, fill out the application form here. We will accept only 12 founders, and maximum 2 candidates from each country.

We look for founders and teams that have proven traction on their home markets, are serious about raising money in Silicon Valley, and are open to learn how to do business the Silicon Valley way.

We evaluate applications on an ongoing basis. We offer a limited number of scholarships funded by the members of Blackbox Investor Club. Eligibility for the scholarship is determined individually for each application.
